The Business Case

Why fair chance hiring wins.

The data is clear. Employers who hire returning citizens don't just do good — they outperform on every metric that matters.

01
Significant Tax Incentives

The federal Work Opportunity Tax Credit (WOTC) provides employers up to $9,600 per qualifying hire from targeted groups — including people released from prison in the past year. Many states layer on additional credits.

Up to $9,600 federal tax credit per qualifying hire under WOTC
02
An Untapped Talent Pool

Over 600,000 people are released from state and federal prisons every year — yet 75% struggle to find work. This is a ready, motivated workforce most employers systematically exclude. Those who hire gain access to exceptional loyalty and work ethic.

600,000+ releases annually — 75% face long-term unemployment without support
03
Higher Retention Rates

Research consistently shows that returning citizens, when given genuine support, stay longer than typical hires. A Johns Hopkins study found higher retention rates than peers without records. Our peer network and transportation support directly reduce costly turnover.

Fair Rise retention: 87% at 12 months vs. 60% food service industry average
